Let's Talk for Education (LTE)

1. Introduction

Bangladesh is a densely populated country and the majority of the people are not aware of the standard education system in our context. The education system in Bangladesh is not functioning well to develop its people with human qualities and professional skills to face real life problems. For this reason, various problems like corruption, lack of discipline, unhealthy social relations etc. are increasing day by day and not a single system is developing properly to serve it’s citizens. In order to face the above problems, the children in Bangladesh should learn norms, values, and behaviors in the schools, which shapes their attitudes and prepare them for future but the disordered educational system, improper course curriculum and many untrained teachers in the educational institutions hardly able to provide required standard education to the students to build them up properly for the country. Though thousands of families are spending much money for their children’s education, it does not worth practically for better education. In many cases people are not aware of the pitfalls in our education system and they do not perform any pragmatic action to root out the pitfalls from this system. The government of Bangladesh, various organizations and individuals all we need to be united to improve our education system. Thus, taking the proper steps to reform the education system is required for the sustainable development of Bangladesh. In order to create a movement to reform our education system, “Let’s Talk for Education (LTE)” is an educational platform which has been initiated for the specific objectives below, supported by Physics and Science Education (PSE) Lab, Department of Physics, Jahangirnagar University.

2. Objectives

  • To connect people and professionals to build educational awareness.
  • To promote social and research activities to improve our education system.

3. Activity

This platform works in the community and educational institutions to attain the objectives are:

  1. To perform Focus Group Discussions (FGD)/ Panel Discussions (PD) and/or seminars among various groups of people and students from school to university.
  2. Necessary surveys are carried out among students and/or in the community to identify various problems in the education system and for their solutions.

4. Organizing Body

It is conducted voluntarily by some dedicated individuals recognized as the member of the LTE.

5. Organizational Rules

  • All members require to participate in biweekly TWO HOURS group study/ FGD to understand the educational problems in Bangladesh and to share/ produce ideas to solve the problems.
  • An activity of LTE (Survey/FGD/PD/Seminar) is led by a member each time which is supported by other members.

6. Funding Process

Internal:In this process donation is deposited directly to the LTE through a bank account. The LTE team takes initiatives to arrange Survey/FGD/PD/Seminar at convenient time and venues. An internal audit is performed to ensure the proper use of the funds for the program. Anyone can see the report through a proper procedure. The fund includes:

  • Personal donations.
  • Agency/ Business group/ Organizational donations.
  • Crowdfunding.

External: In this process the required expenditure for LTE activities (Survey/ FGD/ PD/ Seminar) and relevant cost is arranged externally by any individual/group/agency. LTE team provides necessary budget of the expenditure and intellectual support for the program. LTE team do not handle any cash for the program. In this process donation is not deposited in any bank account .

7. The Impact of LTE

  • Students, and mass people become aware of different educational problems in the system.
  • It increases the peoples’ understanding about the various aspects of the education system such as the proper role of teachers, students, administrators and guardians in the system.
  • It helps individuals to produce desire and dedication to create a voice for education.

8. Frequently Asked Questionnaire

1. What is the goal of the LTE?
Answer: The goal of the LTE is to engage the people to create an education movement.

2. What kind of activities are performed by LTE?
Answer: It conducts Survey/ FGD/ PD/ Seminar on various issues which helps to understand the problems and strength of our education system.

3. What is the age group in the community for which the LTE works?
Answer: It works for all age groups of the people including students from school to university.

4. How do I donate for the LTE?
Answer: Anyone/group/agency can donate directly through bank account according to his/its ability or can arrange LTE programs following external process of funding.
