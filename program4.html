Let's Talk for Education (LTE)

1. Introduction

Bangladesh is a densely populated country and the majority of the people are not aware of the standard education system in our context...

2. Objectives

  • To connect people and professionals to build educational awareness.
  • To promote social and research activities to improve our education system.

3. Activity

  • Focus Group Discussions (FGD), Panel Discussions (PD), and Seminars
  • Community and student surveys to identify problems in education

4. Organizing Body

Voluntarily conducted by members of LTE

5. Organizational Rules

  • Biweekly 2-hour group studies to identify and solve educational issues.
  • Activities led by members and supported by the team.

6. Funding Process

Internal: Donations via bank account, audited for transparency.

External: Individual/group arrangements without handling cash.

7. The Impact of LTE

  • Raise awareness of educational issues
  • Clarify roles of all education stakeholders
  • Encourage public engagement in education reform

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the goal of LTE?
A: To engage people in an educational reform movement.

Q: What activities does LTE conduct?
A: Surveys, FGDs, PDs, and Seminars.

Q: What age groups does LTE target?
A: All age groups, from school students to university level.

Q: How to donate?
A: Via bank deposit or external arrangements.
