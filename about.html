The CERN, Bangladesh

Vision

The CERN, Bangladesh is a research-based educational organization. Our vision is the empowerment of the students to explore the world of nature and engage in critical thinking and understanding for the development of a standard education system to face future challenges in Bangladesh.

Mission

The CERN, Bangladesh works for education and research emphasizing hands-on experiments in science to produce young scientists and understand community problems by applying the principles of science and social sciences.

Our teams conduct various strategic programs. Based on the NCTB syllabus, we teach school and college students through the Community Education Program (CEP) to enhance their understanding and personal development.

The Science Education Program (SEP) conducts hands-on science experiments in different schools to foster critical and analytical thinking.

Through the Self-Education and Learning Program (SELP), our team engages in identifying and solving social problems within the community.

Our team members also present research and organize panel discussions and seminars through a platform called Let’s Talk for Education (LTE), encouraging public involvement in educational reform movements.

All our programs are dedicated to upgrading the education system in Bangladesh to build a better, more informed society.
